Refund Policy

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

At Insurance Packages Network, we are committed to providing transparent and fair refund practices. This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be issued for insurance-related services and transactions processed through our platform.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered under specific circumstances, including but not limited to:

  • Duplicate or accidental payments made by the client.
  • Policy cancellation requests submitted within the applicable cooling-off period.
  • Failure to issue a policy due to underwriting rejection.
  • Errors in billing or incorrect premium charges.

2. Non-Refundable Cases

Certain situations are not eligible for refunds, including:

  • Policies that have already been activated and utilized.
  • Claims that have been filed or processed under the policy.
  • Administrative or service fees where explicitly stated as non-refundable.
  • Late cancellation requests beyond the defined grace period.

3. Refund Processing Time

Once a refund request is reviewed and approved, processing typically occurs within:

  • 5–10 business days for card payments.
  • 7–14 business days for bank transfers.

Processing times may vary depending on financial institutions and payment gateways.

4. Cancellation Policy

Clients may request policy cancellations by contacting our support team. Refund amounts, if applicable, will be calculated based on:

  • Time elapsed since policy activation.
  • Applicable cancellation fees.
  • Insurance provider terms and conditions.

5. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund request, please contact our support team with the following details:

  • Full name and contact information
  • Policy or transaction reference number
  • Reason for refund request

Email your request to info@insurancepackagesnetwork.online.
